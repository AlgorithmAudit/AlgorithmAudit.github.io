We audit on fair algorithms.

What is fair? What are fair algorithms? Based on statistical, legal and economical criteria of fairness, we review whether decisions guided by algorithm are equitable. We develop best practices to deal with fairness at the margin.

About us

We build great companies.
Algorithm Audit combines statistical theory and implemented algorithm. We use a market-driven approach to create long-term value in high technology start-up and scale-up companies.
